accessly documentation
======================

Create accessible plots for the specific needs of your research group and team members.

``accessly`` uses monkey patching to overwrite matplotlib settings to reconfigure plot outputs

Developed for `Code/Astro 2025 <https://semaphorep.github.io/codeastro/>`.
